Special Event Production

DuporDesign provides AudioVisual production services for a wide range of Special Events. We specialize in full-service conceptual planning and execution to ensure your presentation's message is cohesively and clearly communicated, and your audience perceives an enjoyable experience from entry to exit.

  • Fundraising Galas
  • Corporate Presentations
  • Parties & Ceremonies
  • Concerts & Performances

Our in-house event execution services include equipment and personnel to provide:

  • Clear & Articulate Sound
  • Stage Lighting
  • Venue Up-lighting & Decorative Lighting
  • Pipe & Drape (Velour)
  • Custom Gobo Projection
  • Project / Production Management
  • HD Video Projection
  • Broadcast Quality Video Production (IMAG)

  • Broadcast Quality Video Production (IMAG)

Theatrical Event Production

DuporDesign began as a theatrical design and production concept, and continues to provide these services today. Working on theatrical and performance projects allows us to keep our design skills honed and creativity at the cutting edge of available technology. We provide design and execution for Concerts, Theatre, Opera, Ballet, Contemporary Dance, and Multi-Media performance projects.

  • Lighting Design
  • Sound Design
  • Projection Design & Mapping
  • Custom Control Solutions
  • LED Pixel-Embedded Costumes & Props
  • Rigging & Structural Support
  • Stage Management
  • Production / Project Management
